About Me



I'm Deeksha, a passionate data enthusiast driven to solve complex challenges through data science. I have completed my master's in Data Science with a focus on Applied Machine Intelligence from Northeastern University, along with a bachelor's degree in computer science engineering. Currently, I am working as a Data Engineer and have two years of experience as a data analyst.

My journey in data science has been a thrilling exploration of patterns, correlations, and stories hidden within raw data. With a strong background in Computer Science and Data Science, I've developed expertise in Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Modeling, and Data Visualization.

I have hands-on experience in designing scalable data pipelines, implementing solutions like Data Warehousing, ETL processes, and Data Lakes, as well as developing Machine Learning and Deep Learning models.
